Read our story

We've been Making The Delicious Foods Since 1999

Here at Pepper & Spices Restaurant, we believe that food is a celebration of life and that foods are an enduring part of our culture. We challenge ourselves everyday to live up to our name by preparing and serving meals with devotion, elegance, and grace.

Each of us has special memories of food - the kind that keeps us longing for it. Capturing this authentic flavor has become a critical undertaking for Pepper & Spices Restaurant and we are proud to continue to cook meals from recipes perfected from over thousands of years.
